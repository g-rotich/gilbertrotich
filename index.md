## About Me {#about}

I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of South Florida (USF), working on computer vision and machine learning for satellite imagery and remote sensing.

**Research Interests:** Computer Vision; Machine Learning; Deep Learning; Representation Learning; Self-Supervised Learning; Remote Sensing; Satellite Imagery.
